# Prompts Used

## Prompt 1 - Homepage Content

Act as an expert website copywriter.

Create homepage content for Brew Haven Café.

Business Description:

Brew Haven Café is a cozy neighborhood coffee shop that serves handcrafted coffee, freshly baked pastries, and light snacks in a warm and welcoming environment. The café is designed for students, professionals, remote workers, and coffee lovers looking for a comfortable place to work, relax, and socialize.

Include:

1. Hero Section
2. Tagline
3. About Us
4. Our Story
5. Benefits of Visiting Brew Haven Café
6. Why Choose Us
7. Customer Testimonials
8. Final Call To Action

Tone:
Warm, friendly, modern, and professional.
